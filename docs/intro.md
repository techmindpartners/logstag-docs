# Introduction

Welcome to the Logstag Documentation!

Logstag is a modern logging infrastructure designed to make log management easy, scalable, and accessible for everyone.

## What is Logstag?

Logstag helps you:
- Collect logs from various sources.
- Process and transform log data.
- Store and visualize logs efficiently.

## Key Features

- **High Performance**: Built for speed and efficiency.
- **Scalable**: Grows with your infrastructure.
- **Easy Integration**: Works with your existing tools.

Check out the [Getting Started](./getting-started.md) guide to begin your journey with Logstag.
